How To Choose A Strong Password?

  • Use a combination of symbols, numbers, and both uppercase and lowercase letters.
  • Make sure the password length is 8 characters or longer.
  • Avoid using easily guessable information like common words, phrases, or personal details.
  • Consider using passphrases, which are long and memorable sequences of words.
  • Use different passwords for each of your accounts to enhance security.
  • Change your passwords regularly, especially for critical accounts.
  • Consider using a password manager to generate and store strong, unique passwords.
